A Lapland map for sightseeing.


Introduction

Lapland is the largest and northernmost region of Finland. And it borders the Northern Ostrobothnia of Finland in the south, Gulf of Bothnia, Sweden, Norway and Russia. It is as close as reality gets to those who dream of a winter wonderland. It is the home of reindeer, Santa Claus and the Northern Lights.

To present this wonderland, I decide to create this sketch map in the cartoon style with Adobe Sketch and Adobe Illustrator. Cartoon symbols depicts the most featured winter activities or cultures in different regions and their locations.
